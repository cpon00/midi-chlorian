Midi-Chlorian

By: Carter Pon, Adrian Leung, Isaiah Anyimi, and Jason Kalili.

It is a period of quarantine. Computer Science Students, striking from remote classrooms, have begun to take part in the Compilers class.

During the class, a group of students began plans on their ultimate project, Midi-Chlorian, a dynamically typed programming language without the power to destroy an entire planet (unforuntely)

Pursued by procastination, this group of students continue development, creators of the programming language that can save their grade and restore freedom to the galaxy...
